Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic homemade treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Walk over to oven
    • Press the buttons
    • Don't burn yourself (very important)
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes or until edges are nicely browned.
  8. Cool and enjoy!

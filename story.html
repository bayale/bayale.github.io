Chapter 1: The Signal Fades

It was a quiet morning in North Haven. Birds chirped, coffee brewed, and I thought it would be a good idea to toy around with my networking settings.

  • Hardware plugged in and wired ✔
  • DHCP server configured ✔
  • LAN & WLAN settings configured ✔
  • NAS installed ✔
Then, suddenly, the WiFi went out.

Chapter 2: The Search

Panic insued, fear washed over me. Rapidly, I searched for what I broke. The router blinked silently, offering no comfort.

The culprit:

It was the subnet mask settings, I put /24 instead of /23. Whoops. Also the WiFi didn't actually go down, I'm just being dramatic. But I couldn't connect to my NAS and that was moderatly frustrating.

Aside:

Some would suggest I just read a manual or watch a video first, but where's the fun in that!

A modern tale of survival in a disconnected world.

Chapter 1: The Signal Fades

It was a quiet morning in suburbia. Birds chirped. Coffee brewed. Then, suddenly, the Wi-Fi dropped.

Chapter 2: The Search

Desperate, Alex wandered the house, laptop in hand, scanning for any bars of hope. None came. The router blinked silently, offering no comfort.

Reflection

In the absence of Wi-Fi, something unexpected happened: Alex picked up a book, spoke to neighbors, and even went for a walk. Life, it turns out, still worked offline.

Fun Fact:

The average person checks their phone 96 times a day. Alex didn’t check it once that afternoon.
